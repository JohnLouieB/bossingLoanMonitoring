BYLAWS AND LENDING POLICIES


ARTICLE I: MEMBERSHIP AND CONTRIBUTIONS


Section 1: Monthly Contribution
Every member is required to pay a fixed monthly contribution of 300.00.


Section 2: Penalty for Late Contribution
Failure to pay the monthly contribution on the scheduled due date shall incur a Penalty of 5% per month based on the unpaid contribution amount.


Section 3: Withdrawal of Membership
If a member voluntarily exits or withdraws from the group before the fiscal year ends, they shall be entitled to receive only:
Their current year’s total contributions.
Their accumulated contributions and dividends from previous years.
Note: The member forfeits their share of the dividends (income) for the current unfinished year.


Section 4: Annual General Assembly
Attendance at the year-end meeting (Annual General Assembly) is mandatory for all members. Failure to attend the meeting shall incur a Non-Attendance Fine of Php 100.00.


ARTICLE II: LENDING POLICIES


Section 1: Interest Rates
Loans are subject to the following monthly interest rates, calculated based on the Principal Amount (original loan amount):
Member-Borrower: 3% per month.
Non-Member Borrower: 5% per month.


Section 2: Penalty for Late Interest Payment
Failure to pay the monthly interest on the scheduled due date shall incur a Daily Penalty of 2%, calculated based on the outstanding unpaid interest.


Section 3: Loan Term and Validity
All loans are valid for a maximum period of one (1) year. The principal amount must be fully settled upon maturity.


Section 4: Default and Automatic Deduction
For Member-Borrowers: In the event of default (failure to pay within the one-year period), the total outstanding balance shall be automatically deducted from the member’s Total Equity (Current Contributions + Previous Contributions + Accumulated Dividends).
For Non-Member Borrowers: In the event of default, the total outstanding balance shall be charged against the Co-maker, subject to the provisions in Article III.


ARTICLE III: CO-MAKER GUIDELINES


Section 1: Requirement
All Non-Member Borrowers are strictly required to secure a Co-maker who is an active member of the group in good standing.


Section 2: Liability
The Co-maker must acknowledge full liability for the loan. If the Non-Member Borrower fails to pay the monthly interest or the Principal Amount within one year, the Co-maker assumes the obligation to settle the total outstanding balance immediately. This may be done through cash payment or automatic deduction from the Co-maker's own capital and dividends.


Section 3: Limit on Active Loans
A member may act as a Co-maker for a maximum of three (3) active loans simultaneously. They must wait for one of these existing loans to be fully paid before they are eligible to sign as a Co-maker for a new borrower.


ARTICLE IV: DELINQUENCY AND DISCIPLINE


Section 1: Warning System
A Warning is issued for every instance of late payment.
One (1) Late Payment = 1 Warning.
Accumulation of Warnings: Any borrower who accumulates three (3) warnings shall be officially classified as a Delinquent Borrower.


Section 2: Sanctions for Delinquent Borrowers
A member classified as a Delinquent Borrower is suspended from applying for any new loans. This suspension remains in effect until the debt is paid, plus a one (1) month cooling-off period after full settlement.


Section 3: Blacklisting
Any borrower who refuses to settle their obligation despite repeated demands, or who defaults on the one-year loan term without a valid repayment plan, shall be Blacklisted. A Blacklisted individual is Permanently Disqualified from accessing future loans and, if a member, may be subject to expulsion from the group.


ARTICLE V: DISTRIBUTION OF INCOME
Section 1: Net Income Allocation             At the end of the fiscal year, the Group’s Net Income (total collected interest and penalties) shall be distributed in the following order of priority:
1. ​Treasurer’s Honorarium: 10% of the Net Income shall be awarded to the Treasurer as compensation for service.
2. ​Top Agent Incentive: 5% of the Net Income shall be awarded to the declared "Top Agent" (see Section 2).
3. ​Member Dividends: The remaining 85% of the Net Income shall be distributed among all members as Dividends, prorated based on their total share capital/contributions.
​Section 2: Criteria for Top Agent        The "Top Agent" is defined as the member who has served as a Co-maker for the highest total value of Non-Member loans, subject to the following "Good Payer" conditions:
* ​Only loans that were paid on time are counted.
* ​Loans that incurred a "Warning" (late payment) or "Delinquency" status do not count toward the agent's score.
* ​In the event of a tie between two agents, the 5% incentive will be split equally between them.
ARTICLE VI: ACCOUNTABILITY OF OFFICERS


Section 1: Treasurer’s Liability and Misconduct
Any Treasurer (or officer) found guilty of misappropriation of funds, theft, or failure to account for the group’s money shall be subject to Immediate Expulsion from the group.


Section 2: Restitution and Automatic Deduction
In the event of missing funds, the total amount of the loss shall be automatically deducted from the Treasurer’s Total Equity (comprising all Current Contributions and Previous Yearly Contributions).


Section 3: Forfeiture and Refund
Upon expulsion, the Treasurer forfeits all rights to Dividends and Honorarium. They shall only be entitled to receive the remaining balance of their contributions after the missing funds have been fully covered.
